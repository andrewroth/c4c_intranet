Model View Controller (MVC)

Let's take a break for a second and talk about "WHY" we are making RowManagers and ListIterators.

Model View Controller, is a way of breaking up the design of your program into more logical pieces. Rather than try to explain it here, I'll simply refer you other great explanations on the web: ooTips, googleList

The important thing to note, is that we are attempting to incorporate MVC into the design of our framework. In our design, RowManagers and ListIterators fit into the Model portion of the MVC design.

Any part of your program that wants to access the data in a DB table, needs to go through a RowManager. We should not find randome SQL statments strewn about in your code.

(after doing that for 4 years and trying to maintain systems like this, I'm now a firm believer ... use the RowManagers)
